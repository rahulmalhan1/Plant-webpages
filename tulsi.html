Tulsi (Holy Basil)

Ocimum tenuiflorum

Description

Tulsi is a sacred aromatic plant in the mint family, native to the Indian subcontinent. It is highly revered in Hinduism and is considered an "elixir of life" in Ayurveda for its wide-range of health-promoting properties.

Medicinal Uses

  • Used in traditional medicine to treat common colds, coughs, and fevers.
  • The essential oil has calming effects and is used in aromatherapy to reduce stress.
  • Its leaves are often used to make herbal tea, a popular health tonic.
  • Contains powerful antioxidant, anti-inflammatory, and antimicrobial properties.
